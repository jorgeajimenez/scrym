# User Persona: Defensive Coordinator (DC)

**Role:** The Counter-Puncher.
**Environment:** Reactive. Needs to anticipate opponent moves instantly.
**UX Requirement:** **"The Radar"** - Alerts and warnings. High contrast.

## Core Use Cases

### 1. Tendency Alert (Pre-Snap)
**Goal:** Predict the play type based on offensive formation.
- **Workflow:**
    1.  Offense lines up in "Empty Set".
    2.  **UI:** Flashes a bright warning: **"⚠️ WATCH SCREEN"**.
    3.  **Action:** DC yells "Watch the Screen!" to the linebackers.

### 2. Formation Counter
**Goal:** Match offensive personnel without counting heads manually.
- **Workflow:**
    1.  Offense subs in players.
    2.  **UI:** Displays **"MATCH: NICKEL"** (5 DBs).
    3.  **Action:** DC signals the Nickel package onto the field.

### 3. 4th Down Defense
**Goal:** Know if they are bluffing.
- **Workflow:**
    1.  Offense leaves offense on the field on 4th & 1.
    2.  **UI:** Displays probability bar: **"FAKE PROB: 85%"**.
    3.  **Action:** DC calls the "Safe" defense instead of Punt Return.
